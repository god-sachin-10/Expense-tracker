About Our Shop

With over a decade of hands-on experience, Hari OM Electrical and Electronic Repair has been the trusted local hub for fixing all things electronic. We started with a simple mission: to reduce electronic waste by providing affordable, high-quality repairs.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives us to stay updated with the latest technological advancements. Whether it's a shattered smartphone screen or a complex industrial PCB, our skilled technicians approach every job with precision and care.
